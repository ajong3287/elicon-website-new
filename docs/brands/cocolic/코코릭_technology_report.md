## 2. 주요 렌즈 기술 분석


### 2.x. 고굴절률 렌즈 (High-Refractive Index Lenses)
*   **설명/목적**: High-refractive index lenses are a significant advancement in ophthalmic optics, primarily offering thinner and lighter alternatives to traditional lenses, especially beneficial for individuals with high prescriptions. Key aspects include chromatic aberration, material science (plastic, glass, polymer), and coating technologies.


### 2.x. 비구면 렌즈 (Aspheric Lenses)
*   **설명/목적**: Aspheric lenses are effective in improving optical system performance and reducing size and weight by reducing aberrations and providing sharper images. Manufacturing involves precise grinding and polishing. They offer improved flexibility in optical system design for aberration correction.


### 2.x. UV 차단 렌즈 (UV Protection Lenses)
*   **설명/목적**: UV protection lenses shield the eyes from harmful UV radiation, which can cause ocular diseases. They work by incorporating UV absorbers into the lens material or applying UV-blocking coatings. Research shows their effectiveness against UV-induced visual decline and highlights the importance of comprehensive UV protection.


### 2.x. 블루라이트 차단 렌즈 (Blue Light Filtering Lenses)
*   **설명/목적**: Blue light filtering lenses are marketed for reducing eye strain and protecting against blue light from digital devices. However, systematic reviews suggest limited short-term benefits for visual fatigue and unclear long-term effects on macular health, though some studies point to potential benefits for sleep quality under specific conditions.


### 2.x. 기능성 코팅 (Functional Coatings for Lenses)
*   **설명/목적**: Functional coatings significantly enhance lens performance and durability. This includes Anti-Reflective (AR) coatings to eliminate reflections, Anti-Scratch coatings for durability, and Anti-Fog coatings to prevent fogging. Nano-coated solutions are also emerging for maintenance.
